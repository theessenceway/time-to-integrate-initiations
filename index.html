The pattern

It keeps Coming Back

"Time to integrate."

We hear it everywhere we go at every transformational event and even the not so transformational ones. Like a good old song that keeps repeating in our minds, never really knowing what to do with it. We all know that somehow it deeply matters that all the insights and breakthroughs lose their meaning if we don't integrate and embody them. And yet we have never quite received an answer to the most obvious question: what does that actually mean? How do we even integrate?

So we do what we know. We reflect. We journal. We turn to breathwork to find our way back to a clarity we can still taste. Some of us walk into a therapist's office, which takes its own kind of courage. Others go back for another ceremony, willing to face ourselves again, trusting that this time it lands differently. We show up for all of it because some part of us refuses to settle for less than what we know we're capable of. And each time, something real does move. We confront the parts of ourselves that ask to be seen, and arrive somewhere true, a deeper knowing of who we are, what we're here for, how to move forward in alignment with what matters most, so much potential revealing itself, ready to move through.

And yet when we look at our actual lives, we realise that the vision which once felt so alive and clear now quietly waits in the background, carrying a life we know is possible, yet somehow never quite seem to step into. The direction blurs the moment life picks back up. The breakthrough fades into the background of the same patterns, the same hesitations, the same familiar pull away from what we know we are here to do.

After a dozen unfinished projects and moments of almost, we start to doubt whether we can live up to our potential. And even worse, we begin to question if any of it is even real or if we are just delusional and it only exists in those moments of expansion.

And still beneath all of it something keeps returning. That same pull. That same knowing. Too consistent to ignore. Too alive to dismiss.

Maybe the question was never whether it was real. Maybe the question was always why it hasn't landed yet.

The root

The Missing Piece

That knowing of what you're truly capable of, the one that surfaces in the moments you feel most untethered, most yourself, most free of the roles and patterns you normally operate from, is your essence coming through. The deepest and most true part of you, the part that knows exactly what you're here to create and how to bring it to life.

What keeps it from fully coming through are the limiting patterns. Showing up as the fears that surface the moment you sit down to work on what matters most. The patterns that have you second guessing every intuitive decision. The deep rooted beliefs that push you out of alignment right when you are closest to breaking through.

To integrate is to look at what's actually keeping you from acting from your essence, the limiting patterns and imprints in the way, and clear them at the root.

So the vision you've been carrying can belong in your everyday lived experience, with your passion projects being birthed into life with a deep sense of ease and certainty, because finally we have arrived at that sweet spot where our contractions are transformed into expressions of life just flowing through. Full surrender to the knowing within, guiding us throughout.

"Like a seed that already carries the blueprint of the tree it will become it does not need to figure out how to grow. It simply needs the right conditions for what is already inside it to express itself fully."

The method

Why This Works So Deeply

Everything you've tried has moved something. And still, the limiting patterns return. That is because most modalities work with what is on the surface, the thoughts, the emotions, the behaviours. This work goes to where they originate.

The limiting patterns that express themselves as fears, self-sabotage, and the constant pull away from what you know you're here to do have their first expression in the energetic field, long before they become a feeling, a thought, or an action. Their origin reaches far beyond what you are consciously aware of accumulated across lifetimes, through the lineage, and beyond. Silent contracts made in another time and under other circumstances, still quietly governing the choices you make today.

This is where energy medicine works most powerfully. We locate where the imprint resides in your energetic field tracing it back to the original experience that brought it to life, the one still shaping how you see yourself, what you believe is possible, and how far you allow yourself to go and clear it at the root. Through soul retrieval, extraction, illumination, and cord cutting, we retrieve what was lost, remove what doesn't belong, cut what no longer needs to bind, illuminate what has been living in the dark, and seed your essence back into every cell of your being. Deep intuitive bodywork supports and integrates what shifts energetically, working with the fascia to release what has been stuck, allowing a full reset of the nervous system so the clearing can land and be held by the body.

Within ceremonial space, held by Santa Maria, all of this goes deeper and reaches further. She opens what is ready to move and amplifies what is ready to be cleared reaching in a day and a half what might otherwise take months.

It wasn't until a handful of initiatory experiences in Luxor, Egypt, that something clicked. I finally had clarity, a total understanding of what real transformation actually requires. And where it has to go, beyond anywhere the other work had taken me.

The container

What Is Time to Integrate?

Time to Integrate is a day-and-a-half immersed in nature, away from the noise, the roles, and the demands of everyday life, dedicated entirely to you and what is ready to finally move through you and into your life.

You arrive the evening before and are welcomed onto a beautiful piece of land surrounded by nature. The ceremonial space is held beneath an ancient tree. A comfortable caravan is yours to rest and sleep in. Nourishing meals aligned to the experience are prepared for you. Everything is taken care of so from the moment you arrive, your only focus is being.

The following morning, we open the space and move through the full arc of the experience together, supported by Santa Maria, Cacao, and Rapé, held in sacred space, with the land as your foundation throughout. You stay the night. And the morning after is held for final integration grounding what has shifted, clarifying what is calling you forward, before you step back into daily life.

Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

I have never worked with plant medicine before. Is this still right for me?

This experience is designed for people who have done meaningful inner work, whether or not that includes plant medicine. If this is your first time in a ceremonial space, we will explore together in the Alignment Call whether you and the medicine are ready to meet. Safety and readiness come first. Nothing moves that isn't meant to move.

What does the Alignment Call actually involve?

It is a 30-minute conversation, just the two of us. We talk about where you are, what keeps returning, and what you are genuinely ready to move. There is no pitch and no pressure. The call exists to feel whether this is the right container for you at this moment. If it is, we take the next step together. If it isn't, I will tell you honestly.

Do I need to travel to Portugal?

Yes. The experience takes place on a specific piece of land in Santa Clara, Alentejo. The land is part of the work. Being away from your everyday environment, held in nature, is not incidental. It is what makes this possible.

Is this safe?

This work is held with deep care and clear structure. In the Alignment Call we explore your physical and mental health, your readiness, and your relationship to the medicines so that what we step into together is genuinely right for you. The ceremonial space, the medicines, and the process have been prepared to support your system at every stage.

I am already in therapy or other healing work. Can I still come?

Yes. This work does not compete with other modalities. It goes to where they leave off. Many people who come have rich histories with therapy, breathwork, yoga, and ceremony. This experience meets what those have been preparing.

I notice this is the first time you are offering this specific experience. Should I be concerned?

Energy medicine, somatic bodywork, and ceremonial space are methods I have been working with for years across individual sessions and group containers. Time to Integrate is the first time they have been woven into this specific form. You are not a first attempt. You are the first to step into what I have spent years building toward.
